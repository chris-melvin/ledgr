We're excited to launch the official ledgr blog! This is where we'll share practical advice, product updates, and stories from our community.

## What to Expect

Our blog will cover topics that matter to everyday Filipinos managing their finances:

- **Budgeting tips** — Simple, actionable strategies for stretching your daily allowance
- **Product updates** — New features, improvements, and what we're building next
- **Community stories** — Real experiences from ledgr users who've found their rhythm
- **Financial literacy** — Breaking down concepts like emergency funds, debt payoff, and saving goals

## Why We Built ledgr

Most budgeting apps are designed for people who already have their finances figured out. They're packed with features, charts, and categories that feel overwhelming when you're just trying to answer one question: *"How much can I spend today?"*

ledgr starts with that question. You set a daily allowance, log what you spend, and we show you where you stand — clearly, honestly, and without judgment.

> The goal isn't perfection. It's awareness.
